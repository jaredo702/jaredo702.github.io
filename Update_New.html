An update to your system has been requested by your administrator.

You can defer this update a maximum of three times; after the third deferral, it will be installed automatically.

  • You can set a date and time for this update by choosing "Schedule Restart."
  • You can install the update right away by choosing "Restart Now."
  • You can postpone this update by choosing an option from the drop-down menu and then clicking "Defer."

For questions or issues, contact the OTS Help Desk at 702-651-4357.
