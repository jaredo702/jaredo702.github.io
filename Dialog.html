This upgrade/update is a security requirement for all Mac devices at CSN.

This update will be installed automatically and restart your computer after 14 days from today.

You can choose how to install the upgrade by selecting one of the following:

  • Select "Restart Now" to install immediately.
  • Defer the installation (up to a maximum of 4 times) by selecting an option within the "Defer software update for" drop-down menu.
  • Schedule an installation date and time by selecting "Schedule Restart" up to 14 days from today.

All options outlined above will force your computer to reboot. Please save all important data to OneDrive.
