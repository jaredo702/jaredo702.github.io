An update to your system has been requested by your administrator.

This update will be installed automatically, and your computer will restart on or after the specified date above.

The available options are outlined below:

  • You can set a date and time for this update to install by choosing "Schedule Restart."
  • You can install the update right away by choosing "Restart Now."
  • You can postpone this update by choosing an option from the drop-down menu and then clicking "Defer."

You can defer this update a maximum of four times; after the fourth deferral, it will be installed automatically.
